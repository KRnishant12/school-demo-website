About Us

Welcome to RJL Public School

RJL Public School stands as a beacon of educational excellence, dedicated to nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders. With a legacy spanning 25 years, we have consistently delivered quality education combined with character development.

Vision

To empower every student with knowledge, skills, and values to become responsible global citizens.

Mission

Provide holistic education that develops intellectual, physical, and moral dimensions of personality.

Principal's Message

"Education is not just about academics; it's about building character, fostering creativity, and instilling values. At RJL, we believe in nurturing every child's potential to become a responsible and productive member of society." - Dr. Rajesh Kumar, Principal

Our Facilities

Smart Classes

Interactive digital learning with projectors and smart boards in every classroom for enhanced teaching and learning experience.

Central Library

Well-stocked library with 10,000+ books, e-journals, and digital resources for research and recreational reading.

Science Labs

Modern physics, chemistry, and biology labs equipped with latest equipment for practical learning and experimentation.

Computer Labs

Two state-of-the-art computer labs with high-speed internet for coding, web development, and digital literacy.

Transport Facility

GPS-enabled buses covering 50+ routes with trained drivers and attendants for safe student transportation.

Cafeteria

Hygienic food services with nutritious meals prepared by professional chefs following food safety standards.

Sports Complex

Indoor & outdoor sports facilities including basketball, badminton, volleyball, cricket ground, and gym.

Medical Clinic

On-campus medical facility with qualified nurses and doctors for student health and emergency care.
